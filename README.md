# Feelin Sealy 
Seals are often sought to be peaceful creatures, but they have had enough... 

The seals have mobilized to take their land (everyhwere) back and make it Whimiscal. 

They are hungry for destruction and slapping their bellies. 

## Controls: 
<p> Click and Drag to Shoot 

<p> Press W,A,S, and D to Dash in a direction.
<p> You may dash twice per Seal</p>

## Destroying Towers 
<p>Destroy towers and hit the green goal to continue</p>

##Difficulties 

<p>Easy: The seals in Antartica, everything is flat</p>

<p>Medium: The seals are visting Buckingham Palace. They must utilize the dash in order to effectively hit the goal</p>

<p>Hard: The seals are going to space! Things get funky in space.</p>
